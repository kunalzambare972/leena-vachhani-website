Laboratory

• Developed a research laboratory on Embedded Control Systems, which is also utilized in deliver- ing laboratory component of SC700 course. The research in this lab concentrates on designing embedded controllers considering minimal sensing and/or coarse actuation. The students in SC 700 were given hands-on exposure to various embedded platforms. They study and analyze the limitations of these platforms. The experiments are designed to identify and analyze the effect of these limitations, when the embedded platform is used as a controller. Currently, the SC700 course is suspended due to non-availability of course material. It would be offered once the book on embedded control is published.

• Developed a laboratory on Mobile robotics to supplement the course on ”Advanced topics in robotics”. This laboratory has a in-house developed motion capture system using ceiling mounted web-cams. The laboratory experiments are designed in modules to solve various problems defined for mobile robotic applications.

• Developed a unique facility in IIT Bombay for conducting experiments on multiple aerial and ground robots in collaboration with Prof. Arpita Sinha, Systems and Control Engg. This lab- oratory is equipped with ceiling cameras and associated software that can accurately measure the movement of the ground and aerial robots so that motion planning and control algorithms can receive location feedback. This facility supports bench-marking our controller designs as the measured feedback is accurate to be considered as ground truth.

• Next plan is to facilitate experiments for underwater robotic platforms and interconnected system of multiple robots in land, water and air.
